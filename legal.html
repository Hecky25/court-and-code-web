Payment Terms

Work will not begin until a signed contract and full payment of the applicable one-time fee have been received. No exceptions. The website will not go live until the client has fulfilled all outstanding payment obligations for that tier.

For the Pro tier, the setup fee is due in full before work begins. Monthly recurring payments then begin on the date the website goes live and continue on a month-to-month basis for the duration of the 12-month agreement. Invoices not paid within 15 days of the due date may result in a suspension of services until the account is current.

For the Club tier, the one-time setup fee is due in full before work begins. Monthly recurring payments begin on the date the website goes live.

All prices are listed in USD. Court & Code reserves the right to update pricing at any time; however, existing clients on active plans are not subject to price increases during their current 12-month agreement term.

Annual domain renewal is managed and paid by Court & Code on the client's behalf. The client will be invoiced for the renewal cost via Stripe and is responsible for prompt reimbursement. Failure to reimburse within 15 days of the renewal invoice may result in service interruption.
